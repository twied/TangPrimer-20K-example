The ext board comes with six orange LEDS located at the right side of the
board. They are labeled "5" to "0" from top to bottom. The example displays a
simple static pattern.

Note that the LEDs are **active low**, i.e. writing `1` will turn an LED
**off** and writing `0` will turn the LED **on**.
