The ext board comes with a PT8211 two channel DAC, connected to a 3.5 mm audio
jack in the top right corner. It expects data in form of two 16 bit, signed,
MSB words per sample, one for the right and one for the left channel.

This example uses a mono audio clip with 8k samples per second.
